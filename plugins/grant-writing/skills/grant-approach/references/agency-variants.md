# Approach Variants by Funder

## NIH
The per-aim template in SKILL.md is the NIH default. Factor 2 (Rigor and Feasibility) scoring means reviewers explicitly look for: scientific rigor of design, appropriate statistics and power, consideration of relevant biological variables (including sex), authentication of key resources (separate attachment), and feasibility given preliminary data and timeline. Human subjects and vertebrate animal detail goes in dedicated forms/sections, not the Approach — but the design summary in the Approach must be consistent with them.

## NSF
The 15-page Project Description has no mandated internal structure except: Broader Impacts must appear as a separately labeled section, and Results from Prior NSF Support (up to 5 pages within the 15) is required for any senior person with NSF funding in the past 5 years — covering both Intellectual Merit and Broader Impacts of the prior award. A common structure: Introduction/Objectives → Background → Research Plan by objective (rationale, methods, expected results, contingencies) → Broader Impacts → Timeline/Management. Education/outreach plans need the same rigor as the science: named partners, assessment plan.

## DoD
Organize around the Statement of Work when one is required: numbered tasks and subtasks, each with entrance/exit criteria, deliverables, and schedule. Many BAAs want a risk matrix (technical risks × likelihood × mitigation). For DARPA-style proposals, structure the technical approach to answer the Heilmeier catechism: What are you trying to do? How is it done today and what are the limits? What is new and why will it succeed? Who cares / what difference will it make? What are the risks? How much will it cost and how long? What are the mid-term and final exams (metrics)? Quantitative metrics for each phase are expected, not optional.

## Foundations
Approach sections are shorter and less methods-dense; feasibility is often argued through team track record rather than protocol detail. Keep a full-rigor internal version — many foundations request detail at a later stage, and the internal version feeds progress reports.
